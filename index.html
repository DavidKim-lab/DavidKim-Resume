David Kim is a Pre-Business student currently attending UCR. He is working on marketing and graphic design for the needs of individual clients and has experience with freelance and commissions relating to illustrations. He has worked on album covers and logos for very small musicians along with illustrations for commissioners. David constantly strives to improve upon himself and the work he produces. He is incredibly passionate about inspiring others and expressing himself, with each day being an opportunity to produce something to contribute to the world. In his freetime, David enjoys playing piano, listening to music, playing tabletop games with friends, doodling in his sketchbook, and daydreaming.

David is currently working on creating a portfolio for industry work and plans to become a full-time freelancer.
